       The user is first prompted for their old password, if one is present. This password is then encrypted and[m

       both are required to match in order for the password to be changed.[m
[m
       Then, the password is tested for complexity. As a general guideline, passwords should consist of 6 to 8[m
       characters including one or more characters from each of the following sets:[m
